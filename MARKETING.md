### Reddit Post (r/MMORPG / r/incremental_games / r/RuneScape)

**Title:** [Dev] Abyssal Assets — A cryptid hat trading sim with a real CLOB, alchemical transmutation, and 30+ Loch Ness monsters. Think Grand Exchange meets full alchemy system.

**Body:**
Hey all! I've been building this for a while and wanted to share.

**Abyssal Assets** is a browser-based multiplayer hat trading simulator that started as "what if RuneScape's Grand Exchange had a full alchemical backing system?"

**Core Loop:**
1. **DREDGE** — Sonar mini-game to extract materials from the Loch depths
2. **HUNT** — Track and take down 30+ cryptids (Loch Trout → NESSIE → LILITH)
3. **CRAFT** — Hydrothermal Vent Forge: Nigredo→Albedo→Citrinitas→Rubedo transmutation
4. **TRADE** — Real CLOB order book with WebSocket price feeds, market makers, 3% fees
5. **ASCEND** — 12 Acts through Sephirotic zones (Malkuth→Kether), 25 skills with synergies

**What makes it different:**
- **Real CLOB** — Not a fake auction house. Per-item order books, live bids/asks, market maker bots, anti-manipulation circuit breakers
- **Progenitor Tags** — Every hat remembers its origin: the specific monster, the critical hit, the party that killed it. World-first kills get eternal auras.
- **Alchemical Skill Web** — 25 skills across 7 categories. Dredging reveals monsters. Hunting tracks them. Lore reveals drop tables. Bone Carving uses their bones. Everything connects.
- **The Meme Items** — "Tree Fiddy" ($3.50, +0.35 dredge luck, -3.50 sanity). "BEZOS BRAND™ SINGLE-PLY REGRET" (negative clout, satirical). Nessie's Toll (3.50% Monster Tax on transactions).

**Tech:** Phaser 3 + TypeScript frontend, FastAPI + Python 3.11 backend, Socket.io WebSockets, PostgreSQL/Redis, Docker deploy. Integrates with existing sovereign AI infrastructure (no cloud deps).

**Current State:** Phase 2/6 — Core loop playable. Dredging mini-game, inventory, basic crafting, market MVP working. Build passes.

Happy to answer questions about the economy design, alchemy system, or monster ecosystem!
