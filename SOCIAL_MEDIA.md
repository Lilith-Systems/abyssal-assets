## 📝 PRESS RELEASE

### For Immediate Release

**ABYSSAL ASSETS ANNOUNCES PLAYTEST FOR "THE LOCH EXCHANGE" — A CRYPTID HAT TRADING SIMULATOR WITH REAL CLOB ECONOMY AND ALCHEMICAL PROGRESSION**

**SEATTLE, WA** — Independent developer Lilith Systems today announced open playtest signups for *Abyssal Assets: The Loch Exchange*, a browser-based multiplayer cryptid hat trading simulator that combines RuneScape-style Grand Exchange mechanics with a full four-phase alchemical transmutation system.

**What Makes Abyssal Assets Unique:**

**Real Central Limit Order Book (CLOB) Economy**
Unlike traditional MMO auction houses, *Abyssal Assets* implements a genuine CLOB per item — live WebSocket order books with market maker bots, depth charts, circuit breakers, and 3% transaction fees (1% burned, 2% to Nessie's Treasury). Discontinued "Vault" items create true digital scarcity.

**Four-Phase Alchemical Progression**
Players ascend through Nigredo (Grey) → Albedo (White) → Citrinitas (Purple) → Rubedo (Gold), mapping to the Sephirotic Tree's seven zones from Malkuth to Kether. Twenty-five skills across seven categories (Gathering, Processing, Crafting, Knowledge, Social/Economic, Combat/Survival, Metaconscious) form an interdependent web with synergy bonuses.

**Progenitor Tags: Every Hat Has a History**
Every hat records its complete provenance: the specific monster killed, the critical hit that secured it, the party members present, and the creator's skill levels. World-first kills receive eternal unique auras — provenance without blockchain speculation.

**30+ Cryptid Bestiary with Weighted Drop Tables**
From Tourist Debris (Tier 0) to Lilith True Form (Tier 6), each monster has a weighted drop table. NESSIE (Adult) drops Legendary hats at 75% rate. Lilith True Form guarantees Lilith's Crown (1/1). First kills, critical hits, party kills, and world events all modify drops.

**The Abyssal Saga: 12 Acts Through the Sephirotic Tree**
Structured as alchemical operations (Nigredo→Albedo→Citrinitas→Rubedo→Projection), the Main Saga takes players from washing ashore with a Soggy Visor to Lilith's Throne Room, where she crowns them: "You are the Ledger." The MASTERING skill unlocks true endgame — crafting any hat, knowing every trade.

**Satire with Teeth**
"Tree Fiddy" ($3.50, +0.35 dredge luck, -3.50 sanity, 3.50% Monster Tax). "BEZOS BRAND™ SINGLE-PLY REGRET" (negative clout). Corporate critique baked into the economy.

**Tech: Sovereign Infrastructure**
Phaser 3 + TypeScript frontend, FastAPI + Python 3.11 backend, WebSocket real-time layer. Zero cloud dependencies — runs on local hardware. Integrates with existing Metaconscious Singularity Node infrastructure.
